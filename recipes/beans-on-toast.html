Cheesy Beans on Toast

SERVES TWO

Ingredients

  • 4 x bread slices
  • 1 x can baked beans (415g)
  • unsalted butter (to serve)
  • 50g mature cheddar, cubed
  • brown sauce (to serve)
  • black pepper (to serve)
  1. Preheat grill to maximum temperature.
  2. Heat the beans in a small saucepan until nearly boiling.
  3. Meanwhile, place bread under grill. Brown both sides. (For extra flavour, lightly drizzle your bread in olive oil and season with salt flakes)
  4. When the beans are nearly at a boil, remove from the heat and stir in the cheese.
  5. Put two slices of toast on each plate and top with the cheesy bean mixture. Add a dollop of brown sauce and a crack of black pepper. If desired, top with herby greens.
