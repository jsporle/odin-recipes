Breakfast Porridge

SERVES TWO

Ingredients

  • 80g porridge oats
  • 300ml semi-skimmed milk
  • 250ml water
  • sea salt flakes
  • chopped fruit (to serve)
  • honey (to serve)
  1. Add oats, milk, water and a pinch of salt to a small pan and bring to a swift boil.
  2. Reduce to a medium-low flame, stirring frequently until the porridge reaches the desired thickness.
  3. Divide into two bowls and top with fruit and a generous drizzle of honey.
  4. Try topping with greek yoghurt and flaked almonds.
