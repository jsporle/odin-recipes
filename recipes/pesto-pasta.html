Pesto Pasta

SERVES FOUR

Ingredients

  • 1 x jar pesto (190g)
  • 400g fusilli
  • 50g parmesan cheese, grated
  • black pepper (to serve)
  • olive oil (to serve)
  1. Add pasta to a medium pan of salted boiling water. Cook per packet instructions.
  2. Drain the pasta. In the same pan, add the jar of pesto and stir through the pasta.
  3. Plate up and add a handful of grated parmesan, a crack of black pepper and a drizzle of olive oil.
  4. Can be served as is or alongside a leafy green salad and tomatoes.
